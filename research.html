Research Projects

Breaking news, breaking barriers?

Principal Investigator. A Fair and Resilient Societies UvA funded project (€40,000) exploring media representations of immigrants through journalistic and citizen lenses, in collaboration with Kathleen Beckers and Maja Hertoghs.

Understanding the Just Transition

Co-Investigator. An ENLENS UvA funded project (€45,000) examining energy transition through wellbeing, justice, and equity perspectives, led by Matteo Fermeglia with Colin Hickey and Christina Eckes.

COVIDEU

Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Humboldt University of Berlin (2022-2023), studying how crises affect the EU, with a focus on political communication, political engagement, and Europeans' attitudes.

Register to Vote

Project Member. Series of digital field experiments on voter registration through social media and text messages in the UK, in collaboration with Florian Foos, Peter John, and Vanessa Cheng-Matsuno.

Multidimensional Policy Responses to COVID-19

Project Member. Collaborative project funded by King's Together Award (£18,424) examining health-wealth preferences and policy implications during the pandemic.
